Hi, My name is Paavo

I'm a sport sciences graduate with degrees focused on biomechanics and the biology of physical activity. During my studies, I developed a strong interest in health technology, which led me to pursue a second MSc in biomedical engineering.

I'm driven by the desire to understand the intricacies of the human body and explore how technological advancements can shape the future of sports and health. I believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to push the boundaries of science and innovation.
