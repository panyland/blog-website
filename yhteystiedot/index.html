Hi, My name is Paavo

I'm an inquisitive individual with a keen interest in sports, health, and technology. I hold a Master’s degree in biomechanics, where I explored the fascinating relationship between human movement and physical performance.

Beyond my academic achievements, I'm passionate about programming and all things tech-related. I aim to leverage these skills to uncover insights that contribute to optimizing athletic performance and improving overall well-being.

I'm constantly driven by the desire to understand the intricacies of the human body and how advancements in technology can shape the future of sports and health. I believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to push the boundaries of science and innovation.

The purpose of this website is to share my knowledge, experiences, and projects as I develop my expertise in this field. I hope you find something interesting here!
