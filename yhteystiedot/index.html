Hi, My name is Paavo

I'm a sport scientist with degrees in biomechanics and biology of physical activity, and I am currently completing my master's studies in biomedical engineering. I have a keen interest in the intersection of sports, health, and technology.

I'm driven by a desire to understand the intricacies of the human body and to explore how technological advancements can shape the future of sports and health. I believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to push the boundaries of science and innovation.

The purpose of this website is to share blog posts, research reviews, and other engaging content in this field.
