Hi, My name is Paavo Nyländen

I'm an inquisitive individual with a keen interest in sports, health, and technology. Currently, I'm pursuing my master's degree in biomechanics, exploring the fascinating relationship between human movement and physical performance.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I'm interested in programming and all things tech-related. I want to leverage these skills to uncover insights that contribute to optimizing athletic performance and improving overall well-being.

As a sports enthusiast, I'm constantly driven by the desire to understand the intricacies of the human body and how advancements in technology can shape the future of sports and health. I believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to push the boundaries of science and innovation.

The purpose of this website is to share my knowledge, experiences, and projects as I develop my expertise in this field. I hope you find something interesting here!
