Hi, I'm Paavo

After completing graduate studies in biomechanics and biology of physical activity, I became increasingly motivated to explore the intersection of sport sciences and technical disciplines. This interest ultimately led me to pursue a second master's degree in biomedical engineering.

I'm driven by a desire to understand the intricacies of the human body and to explore how technological advancements can shape the future of sports and health. I believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to push the boundaries of science and innovation.

The purpose of this website is to share blog posts, research reviews, and other engaging content in this field. Below you'll find some of the courses and topics I have studied.

Biomedical Engineering

  • Bioinformatics and data science
  • Physiological measurements and biosignal analysis
  • Modeling of physiological systems

Biomechanics

  • Motion analysis and musculoskeletal modeling
  • Sports and clinical biomechanics
  • Human neuroscience

Biology of Physical Activity

  • Human anatomy and physiology
  • Exercise physiology and biochemistry
  • Biomechanics and neuromuscular measurements
  • Laboratory fitness testing
  • Strength and conditioning in sports

Miscellaneous

  • Integral and differential calculus
  • Multivariable calculus
  • Probability and statistics
  • Programming, data structures and algorithms
